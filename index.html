BFPVRA 2016 British Championship

October 1-2, 2016

Race event:

The annual British Championship is the flagship event for the BFPVRA and is where the 60 top qualifying pilots from the British Championship Series are invited to compete for the title of British Champion.
For 2016, it was held at Ragley Hall in conjunction with the Festival of Flight and Helifest.

Format:

60 pilots competed in 10 heats of 6.
Five rounds of qualifying, with overall qualifying positions calculated from each competitor's best three scores using the round-by-round (RBR) scoring scheme1.
The top 24 pilots went through to the knockout rounds2.
All races lasted 2 minutes.
For qualifying rounds, race distance was unlimited.
For knockout rounds, race distance was set to 4 laps.

1Round-by-round scoring awards points to each competitor based on their ranking in each round, which is calculated from the number of laps completed and the time taken.
